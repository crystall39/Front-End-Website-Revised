Sleep

This seems like an obvious thing to do when you're bored. Not to mention how tiring school and/or work are (especially with those assignments).*

Benefits of a Good Night's Rest

Most professionals recommend 8 to 10 hours of sleep for teens and 7 to 9 hours for adults. I know most of us aren't getting our hours in. However, when we have the time, we should take some time to at least get a nap in.

If you sleep more, you will get sick less often, which is important because winter is coming. The weather is getting colder and I think we can all agree that being warm under the covers feels comfortable.

Additionally, sleep reduces stress and improves mood. That saying that you will "sleep it off" is true! But if you continue to dream about stressing out over assignments, that's on you. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ **

Furthermore, a good night's rest can improve memory as well as concentration on a topic. I think it's fair to say that if you're tired, you won't remember much of what happened that day.
